Hi! I am currently a researcher at the College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, supervised by Prof. Dusit Niyato (FIEEE).

Prior to this, I obtained a BEng degree at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China, under the guidance of Prof. Kun Wang (SMIEEE, now a professor with Fudan University, China) and an MSc degree at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), USA, under the guidance of Prof. Lei He (FIEEE).

My research is primarily centered around the utilization of Generative AI (GenAI) in intelligent networking. Our key objective is to explore “how the intelligent networks can support various AI-Generated Content (AIGC) services,” and “how GenAI (and LLM) can be used to improve the performance, security, and reliability of next-generation networking.” Please check our group at NTU for more details!

Our research has been published in prestigious networking journals and conferences, including IEEE JSAC, IEEE TMC, IEEE COMST, IEEE TII, IEEE IoTJ, IEEE INFOCOM, ACM FPGA, IEEE ICC, IEEE Globecom, etc.

Also, I serve as the reviewer/TPC member for various journals/conferences, including IEEE TIFS, IEEE COMST, IEEE TSC, IEEE TCOM, IEEE TNSE, IEEE IoTJ, IEEE Globecom’ 24, IEEE/CIC ICCC’ 24, IEEE PIMRC’ 24, IEEE VTC-Fall’ 24, IEEE ICNC’ 25, IEEE GIIS’ 25, IEEE ICASSP’ 25, NeruIPS’ 25 SafeGenAi, ICLR’ 25, AAAI’ 25 UC, IEEE ICC’ 25 etc.
